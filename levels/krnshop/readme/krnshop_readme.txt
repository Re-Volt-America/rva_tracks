Description:
Antigrav is a company that developed anti-gravity technology. It has several applications, such as storage and entertainment.
Food Vault now use their technology for storage, and the Antigrav Shopping Center has an anti-gravity room open for everybody.

The time trial times are for SEMI-PRO cars.

Known issues:
The camera wont be very good when driving on a wall or upside down. It depends on the cars.
