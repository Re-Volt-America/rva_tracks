-----------------------------
---Lego track made by Kiki---
-----------------------------

Race around an eerie aquarium after hours! Precision is key for swerving through the tight corridors neatly and without incident.
Time trial challenge times are set with the Pro class of cars in mind. Author lap times are made with Toyeca and they are 50.130 / 47.990 for regular and reversed versions, respectively.
